Dear Vassar Parents and Families,

As co-chairs of Parent Giving, we welcome parents to the Vassar community for the 2016-2017 academic year.

We’ve experienced first hand the intellectual and personal growth that transforms students who attend this extraordinary college.  And we all, as parents, play an integral role in supporting these young people throughout their Vassar experience.  here are a few ways that you can impact campus life for all Vassar students.
 

  1. Get involved with our Career Development Office (CDO).  Please consider partnering with the CDO, an invaluable resource in helping Vassar students make the challenging transition from college to career.  Whether mentoring a student, sharing your professional experience, offering an internship, or hiring a Vassar grad, you can have a profound impact on the future of a Vassar student. 
  2. Make a Gift!  The Vassar Fund supports every aspect of a Vassar education.  Unrestricted gifts in particular allow the College flexibility in applying resources to the areas greatest need, or greatest opportunity, over the course of each year.  Contributions to the Vassar Fund help meet current expenses for academic program support, library acquisitions, technology, athletics, career counseling and numerous other student-related programs.

Again, welcome to the Vassar Community!  We look forward to meeting you at a Vassar event in the coming months.

Best wishes for the year ahead.

Kary and John McIlwain P’18
Laura Jones and Stephen Hankins ’85, P’13, P’17
Parent Giving Co-Chairs

VASSAR CONNECT COMMUNITY

The Vassar Connect Community, a secure online portal, is a convenient way to search for and communicate with other Vassar Parents. 

To register please go to: parentconnect.vassar.edu and click on “Parent Directory” to begin the activation process.  You will need your unique Vassar ID number.  Your account will stay active throughout your student’s enrollment.  Having trouble logging in?  Email oaadweb@vassar.edu and provide your full name, mailing address and name of your child attending Vassar and a staff member will be able to assist with your request.

Travel

By plane
Stewart Airport is located in Newburgh, NY, approximately 40 minutes by car from the Vassar campus.  Vassar also provides shuttle transportation to the New York City airports, JFK and LaGuardia, four times during the school year.  About a month prior to the October, winter, and spring breaks, the dates and times of the shuttle schedule are sent out in a campus-wide email to all students.  Vassar also provides shuttle service at the end of the academic year.

By train
The Poughkeepsie Train Station is serviced by Amtrak and MetroNorth.  MetroNorth goes south to Grand Central Station in New York City (leaving hourly at 33 minutes past the hour on weekdays, more frequently during rush hours, and at regular intervals on weekends).  Amtrak goes both north to Albany and south to Pennsylvania Station in New York City.  Call for schedule information.

By car
You’ll find directions for driving to campus at info.vassar.edu/visit.

Without a car?
Zipcar has also become a practical alternative to bringing a car to campus.  Students can sign up to use two available cars on campus that are provided by the nationwide Zipcar rental system. Go online to zipcar.com/vassar to register for special rates.
